Essential Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a secure and memorable journey through the incredible landscapes of India.

Documentation & Legal Requirements

Ensure you have all necessary documents before traveling to India:

Valid Passport

Your passport must be valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay in India.

Visa Requirements

Obtain the appropriate visa for your purpose of visit. Tourist visas are available for most nationalities.

Registration

Foreign nationals staying more than 180 days must register with the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO).

Health & Medical Safety

Protect your health during your Indian journey:

Vaccinations

Consult your doctor about recommended vaccinations including Hepatitis A, Typhoid, and Japanese Encephalitis.

Food & Water Safety

Drink bottled or boiled water. Eat at reputable establishments and avoid street food until you're accustomed to local cuisine.

Medications

Carry a sufficient supply of any prescription medications in their original containers with clear labels.

Health Advisory

In case of medical emergency, dial 102 for ambulance services. Major cities have excellent private hospitals with English-speaking doctors. Travel insurance with medical coverage is highly recommended.

Personal Security

Stay safe during your travels with these personal security tips:

Awareness

Stay alert in crowded places like markets, railway stations, and tourist attractions where pickpocketing may occur.

Valuables

Use hotel safes for passports, cash, and valuables. Carry only necessary items when sightseeing.

Solo Travelers

Women traveling alone should dress modestly, avoid traveling alone at night, and inform someone of their whereabouts.

Transportation Safety

Travel securely using India's various transportation options:

Metro Rail

Safe and efficient in major cities. Keep valuables secure and be aware of rush hour crowds.

Buses

Use government or reputable private bus services. Keep your ticket and destination information handy.

Taxis & Rideshares

Use registered taxis or rideshare apps like Ola and Uber. Share your trip details with someone trustworthy.
